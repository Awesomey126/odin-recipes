Lasagna

This is a recipe for a tasty lasagna. I don't know what else you expected. You saw the title of the page. I hope you know that the image is of a lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Italian Sauage
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Dried Basil
  • Dried Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Brown the italian sausage in a pan and season as desired
  3. If you are not useing oven-ready noodles, prepare them as the box directions suggest
  4. Mix desired amout of Basil and oregano into marinara sauce
  5. Mix together ricotta, some mozzarella, grated parmesean, and desried amount of herbs
  6. Pour some sauce on bottom of oven dish
  7. Place lasagna noodles to cover bottom of pan
  8. Pour some more sauce over noodles
  9. Put down a layer of cheese mixture
  10. Sprinkle a lyear of italian sausage and some more mozzarella onto noodles
  11. Repeat previous steps 7-10 until desired height is reached
  12. For final layer Place down noodles, cover with sauce, and finally coverwith mozzarella cheese
  13. Cover with foil and bake until cooked through
  14. Finally remove foil and baked for an additional 5 mins
  15. Let sit at least 15 minutes before serving
